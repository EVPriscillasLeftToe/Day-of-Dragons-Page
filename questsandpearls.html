Quests

By doing quests, you can unlock unique pearls and even a new dragon species. Currently, there are only 3 quests in the game.
It is not yet confirmed if there will be added more.

  • Becoming a Brood Watcher

    • This quest is available at all time for all players.

  • Winters gift

  • Myth of the mimikor

Pearls

There are 3 types of pearls in the game.

  • Passive buff - This kind of pearl adds a buff to your dragon when equiped.
  • Skin overlay - With this type of pearl equiped, your dragon will show another skin.
  • Cosmetic - When equiped, these pearls will summon tiny creatures that fly around your head when laying down or resting.
