Nesting

Nesting is a way to get better stats for your dragon or to pass down skins to your offsprings.
To nest, you will need a male and a female dragon. The male needs to initialize the nesting proces by aiming (rmb) to the female and pressing the courtship (nr.5) emote. To accept the courtship as the female you simply have to aim back at the male and also press the courtship emote. The female now has to aim to the ground (or to a vertical wall as a biolumin) and press N.
The nest will now be placed. If the female continues to lay on the nest, eggs will begin to spawn. Before you can invite another player to the egg, the eggs need to be incubated. Most nests incubate their eggs automatically when at the right temperature, but fire or lightning element nests will need to be charged in order to incubate. This can be done by breathing fire or shooting lightning on the nest, depending on what the nests element is.
To invite a player to an egg, press E to interact with the nest. You will now see the details of it. Click an egg icon at the left of the menu to see the details of the egg. There you can invite others from your group to hatch from it. Only females can invite to eggs.

Skins

In the future, skin crafting will be added wich allows each player to create their own skins. For now, there are multiple skins available wich can be obtained by nesting or supporting the game on Patreon.
Every time you choose to hatch in a random skin from te spawn menu, you have a chance to hatch in a common, uncommon or rare skin. Some skins require a DLC or a certain payment on Patreon and will therefore not have a chance to spawn in with the "random skin" option.
If you have reached the LT15 rewards on Patreon, you will be able to spawn in all uncommon skins and if you have reached LT100, you will be able to spawn in all rare skins. However, the payment on Patreon is not required to spawn these skins in with the "random skin" option.
