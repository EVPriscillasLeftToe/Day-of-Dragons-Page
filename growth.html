Growth

Every species will grow automatically over time. Higher class dragons will require more time to grow than lower class ones. If both your hunger and thirst are below 20% while growing a hatchling or a juvenile, you will gain a buff that increases your growth speed. For hatchlings, speed will be increased by 50% and for juveniles 25%. To continue growth, you must survive. If you die, you lose your current progress and will need to start from scratch.
To survive, you must keep your health above at least 0,1%. In order to do this, you must keep your hunger and thirst below 100% and win or succesfully flee from any fight you come across.

Eldering

Eldering allows you to unlock new buffs and/or abilities.
